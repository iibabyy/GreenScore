About GreenScore

Our Mission

At GreenScore, we're committed to revolutionizing the healthcare industry's approach to environmental sustainability. Our innovative AI-powered platform enables healthcare professionals and organizations to make environmentally conscious decisions about medical products.

What We Do

We provide a cutting-edge web application that:

  • Calculates the environmental impact of medical products
  • Compares different medical solutions based on their ecological footprint
  • Offers sustainable alternatives when available
  • Helps healthcare facilities reduce their carbon footprint

Team Broken

Founded by a passionate group of innovators, Team Broken combines expertise in healthcare, environmental science, and artificial intelligence to create practical solutions for sustainable healthcare practices.

Our Values
